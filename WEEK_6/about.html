Our Strength is Our Taste - since 1967.

 


 I get bored of clothing that don’t stand out for plus-sized individuals. Sure, there are plain clothes and clothing with flowers that get to me, like the one above, BUT it’s not enough for me. I desire those clothes with the million belt buckles. The ones with lace on one end, mesh on another. I want diversity. My inspiration from all this crazy streetwear-aesthetic was from Japanese fashion, typicically the Harajuku culture. They are not afraid to show off what they got, and I want to bring that here in the heart of LA for all of my big lovelies.
