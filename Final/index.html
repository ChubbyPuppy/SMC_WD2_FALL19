Preservation through Education.

Environmental Science Museum & Exotic Wildlife Rescue Facility

OUR GOALS

The STAR Eco Station's mission is "Preservation through Education."
It carries out this mission every day by educating students,
visitors to the STAR Eco Station and community members about the preservation of the environment
and the inter-dependence of all living things.

A WELCOMING COMMUNITY FOR ALL

Public Tour Hours

Guided tours of the STAR Eco Station introduce visitors to over two hundred rescued exotic wildlife, from tropical fish to tortoises, from parrots to pythons. Each tour is led by an Eco Station staff member, and features fun animal facts, hands-on animal encounters, surprising rescue stories, environmental exhibits, and innovative approaches to going green.
